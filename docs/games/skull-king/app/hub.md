# Skull King hub

Route: `/games/skull-king`

The hub is your entry point for Skull King in GUB. Three tiles:

## Play

**Live multiplayer** — each player on their own phone or laptop.

- Best for: 3–8 players, real-time trick play, join codes
- See [Play guide](./play.md)

## Score Calculator

**Tableside scoring** — one device tracks the whole voyage.

- Best for: physical deck at the table, manual bid/trick entry, complex bonus events
- See [Calculator guide](./calculator.md)

## Rules & Docs

**Rules reference** — in-app page today; full markdown library in `docs/games/skull-king/`.

- Best for: learning rules, scoring lookup, artifact options
- Route: `/games/skull-king/docs`

## When to use what

| Situation | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| Everyone has a device, want digital play | **Play** |
| Paper cards, need scorekeeper | **Calculator** |
| Teaching a new player | **Rules** (start with [Overview](../rules/00-overview.md)) |
| Dispute at the table | [Trick resolution](../reference/trick-resolution.md) or [FAQ](../rules/10-faq.md) |
