# Leading specials

What card **leads** determines whether players must follow suit.

## Escape, Tigress-as-Escape, or Loot leads

- The trick has **no suit** until a suited card is played.
- The **next suited card** played sets the follow suit for remaining players.
- If no suited card is played, see escape/loot resolution in [Card ranks](./04-card-ranks.md).

### Loot + all Escapes

If Loot leads and every following card is Escape-like, the **Loot player wins** the trick.

## Mermaid, Pirate, Skull King, Tigress-as-Pirate, Kraken, or Whale leads

- **No follow suit** — each player may play any legal card.
- Character leads behave like “no suit” tricks for follow purposes.

## Suited card leads

- Normal follow-suit rules apply. See [Playing tricks](./03-trick-play.md).
